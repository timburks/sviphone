Adaptive Auto Layout

The modern world of iOS user interface development is more complex than ever before, especially since the introduction of iOS 8 and an ever-increasing variety of devices including the iPhone 6 Plus. Auto Layout and Size Classes are the tools we are supposed to use to control this explosion of complexity, but developers tend to have a love-hate relationship with these technologies.

Tyler Fox, iOS software engineer at Intuit and developer of PureLayout ( https://github.com/smileyborg/PureLayout ), will cover the latest strategies and techniques for creating adaptive user interfaces without losing your sanity. He'll also deep dive on some of the powerful features of PureLayout that will save you time building, debugging, and maintaining your UI layouts.
