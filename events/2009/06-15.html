WWDC Meltdown

Details

Our next meetup is the Monday after WWDC and will be at the Blue Chalk Café in Palo Alto. If you won't be at WWDC, come meet with people who were there, and if you will be at the conference, come and share some of your new magic powers.

Unlike our usual meetups, this time we'll focus on mixing and meeting. We'll open at 7pm and will be requesting
a $5 contribution at the door (you'll get a ticket for a free drink in exchange). We'll break in the middle to hear about some exciting new monetization tools from OfferPal and TapJoy, and we'll get back to our usual presentation-packed format in July.
