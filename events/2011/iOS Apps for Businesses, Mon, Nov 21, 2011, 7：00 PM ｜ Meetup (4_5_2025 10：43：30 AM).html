iOS Apps for Businesses

Details

Thanks to Intuit for hosting and offering pizza for our November meetup. We'll also be starting the evening with a panel of Intuit experts on mobile apps:

D4d: Transforming Small Business with Mobile

Moderator: Avikk Ghose - Strategic Mobile Initiatives, Intuit

Panelists:
Omar Green - Director of Mobile Technology Strategy, Intuit
Joseph Mao - Product Manager, Intuit GoPayment
Joseph O'Sullivan - Director of Mobile Design, Intuit
Siddharth Ram - Chief Architect for Intuit Small Business

Following this we'll have three shorter talks about iOS and business:

Jessica Mah of inDinero will tell us about her web startup and new iOS app for personal financial management (look out Intuit),

Leslie McCue and Sharad Unni of Asurion will describe the personal device management products that Asurion offers its device insurance customers,

and Jess Taylor of Pervasent will tell us about their experiences building apps for large and small enterprises.
