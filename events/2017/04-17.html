At Tile: Bluetooth LE and more

Details

For our April meetup, we'll be getting the latest news on Core Bluetooth and Bluetooth LE from the team at Tile (https://www.thetileapp.com/), makers of elegant Bluetooth LE-based trackers (https://www.thetileapp.com/en-us/products) for personal items.

We have room for a second talk, and as a new user of a Raspberry Pi 3 Model B (https://www.raspberrypi.org/products/raspberry-pi-3-model-b/), I'm wondering if anyone has experience running Bluetooth LE services on these and calling them from Core Bluetooth on Mac or iOS. Want to tell us about this and share a demo project? Send me ideas and proposals: timburks at google.com.

UPDATE: Ramin Firoozye will be giving a second talk on Raspberry Pi and other hardware-related news.

Directions to Tile:

  • Tile is located at 2121 S. El Camino Real, San Mateo, CA 94403 on the 9th floor of the New York Life building

  • Parking - located behind the property and can be accessed at this address: 2112 Palm Ave, San Mateo, CA 94403 (across from the tow yard). The second floor of the parking garage is where you can enter onto the property.

  • Once You Arrive - Please go to the tower in the center of the courtyard and enter through the main door. Use the elevators behind the security desk to go to the 8th floor (the Tile office). If you have any trouble, please let security know that you are trying to find the iOS Meetup event.
