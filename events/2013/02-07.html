Post-Renaissance Demo Party at Apps World

Details

In addition to our regular monthly meetup on February 18, we'll have this special event in San Francisco in collaboration with Apps World.

---

http://photos3.meetupstatic.com/photos/event/3/d/b/a/event_192375802.jpeg

http://photos3.meetupstatic.com/photos/event/4/8/f/a/event_192378682.jpeg

All are welcome to join us on February 7 at 5 p.m. at the Apps World conference in San Francisco for a demo-filled followup to the January Renaissance iOS conference. We'll feature demos of the best apps at Renaissance and include great opportunities for developers, investors, and creative people with ideas to meet and share.

Please Note: to attend this event, you MUST ALSO register here http://www.apps-world.net/northamerica/register/free-developer-track-a-exhibition-pass for free access to the Apps World expo and 2 days of developer workshops.

Apps World is a global multi-platform event that is heading to San Francisco for the first time on 7&8 February 2013. The event hosts 2 days of free developer content over 4 different tracks as well as a huge expo with over 150 exhibitors. Find out more here http://www.apps-world.net/northamerica

http://photos1.meetupstatic.com/photos/event/7/8/a/2/event_191130882.jpeg

Renaissance is a three-day conference on "the art and science of apps" focusing on iOS and addressing the business, design, and technology that app makers need to succeed on the world's leading mobile application platform. Renaissance is happening on January 21-23, 2013 at UCSF Mission Bay Conference Center in San Francisco. For more information, please visit http://renaissance.io

Renaissance is on Facebook http://facebook.com/renaissanceio and Twitter http://twitter.com/renaissanceio .
