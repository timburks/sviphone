Renaissance: the iOS App Makers' Conference

Details

Please note: attendance at Renaissance (including the Demo Party) requires registration through Eventbrite. A meetup.com RSVP is neither required nor sufficient for admission. Register at http://renaissance2013.eventbrite.com .

http://photos1.meetupstatic.com/photos/event/7/8/a/2/event_191130882.jpeg (http://renaissance.io/)

Renaissance is a three-day conference for iOS app makers that is uniquely designed and produced by app makers - and it shows in the sessions.

Renaissance features a fast-paced single track of sessions that address the business, design, and technology of apps, all focused on the ingredients of successful app development.

Renaissance speakers include creators of many successful apps and top talent from areas vital to app success. Several sessions uniquely emphasize the app business: Winning in the App Store on Monday talks with developers making a living from App Store sales, Apps for Hire on Tuesday includes four heads of app development consulting companies, and Wednesday’s Power Up your IP features prolific IP attorney Susan Gibbs, veteran venture capitalist John Malloy, and former Cisco M&A chief-turned-angel investor Dan Scheinman.

Other sessions go deep into animation, audio, testing, and other essentials for a great app user experience. In one titled "Designing Engagement", Black Pixel Director of User Experience Jaimee Newberry will lead with a review of elements of user experience that make or break an app's chances of being regularly used. Then Prismatic's Aria Haghighi will talk about machine learning and user modeling techniques like the ones that his company is using to create personalized and context-aware news reading experiences.

Read more about it at http://renaissance.io .

If you are attending Renaissance and would like to demo one of your iOS apps at our Tuesday evening conference Demo Party, please post a comment below with the name and brief description of your app.
